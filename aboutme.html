About

My family and I are from a small rancho in Mexico called Tarimoro. It is a place where everyone seems like family, the freedom to run around is unbelievable, the parties are untouchable, and the food is always homemade.
I love feeling the sun beat down on my face, the sand between my toes, the wind brushing through my hair, and the water gliding around me.
One of my favorite activities to do in my free time is to go hiking.
One day I want to travel the world and go on endless adventures having the feeling of teetering through the places unknown to me.
